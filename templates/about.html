About the RC7 Officers Yearbook

Preserving identity, memory, and continuity for Regular Course 7 officers.

Purpose

The RC7 Officers Yearbook was created to preserve the identity, record, and legacy of Regular Course 7 officers in a secure digital format.

Why It Was Created

At the time of passing out, it was brought to our attention that a yearbook would be important because of the size of the course and the need for officers to be able to identify one another in the future. However, the hardcopy yearbook was not produced. This platform was therefore created to fill that gap in a more practical and lasting way.

Its Advantage

The RC7 Digital Yearbook retains the core benefits of a traditional hardcopy yearbook while removing many of its limitations. Like a printed yearbook, it serves as a record of identity, course membership, and shared history. Unlike a hardcopy version, it is accessible from anywhere, can be updated over time, and remains useful as officers grow in service and posting.

Access and Trust

This platform is strictly for Regular Course 7 officers. Measures have been put in place to ensure that access is limited to verified RC7 members, so that the information displayed here remains within the course community.

Our Goal

The goal of this platform is not only to preserve memory, but also to strengthen recognition, connection, and continuity among RC7 officers over time.

Disclaimer

This platform is a private initiative created for members of Regular Course 7 (RC7) to help foster connection and maintain professional relationships among coursemates. It is not an official Nigeria Police platform and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or representing the Nigeria Police Force in any capacity. Users are solely responsible for the information they choose to share on this platform. Members are advised not to upload or disclose any sensitive, confidential, or operational information, including but not limited to details of assignments, units, or duties that may compromise personal or organizational security. This platform is intended strictly for personal and professional networking within RC7.
